The glow from his palm intensified, pulsing in rhythm with the distant light. He took a deep breath and stepped off the cliff, not into empty air, but onto a hidden, ancient stairway carved into the rock face. Moss and time had worn the steps smooth. As he descended into the gathering dark, the forest's whispers grew clearer, forming words in a forgotten tongue. The light ahead brightened, revealing the outline of a stone archway, overgrown with vines. The mark on his hand burned, not with pain, but with recognition. He had found the threshold.
